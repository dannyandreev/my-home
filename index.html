About Us
The Light House is located in an old victorian neighborhood at 126 Jordan Street, a five minute walk from downtown Santa Cruz. Living here is a community of outdoorsy, kind, and motivated people. Students, surfers, engineers, film producers and entrepreneurs have called the Light House home over the past few years and we pride outselves in easy-going, friendly atmosphere of our home.
Looking forward to having you as our new housemate!
